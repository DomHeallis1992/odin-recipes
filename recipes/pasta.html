Dinner at Sea

Seafood pasta made with:

  • Mussles
  • Shrimps
  • Red Wine Tomato Sauce

The seafood is cooked seperately and tossed into the red wine sauce near finish.

Ingredients

  • Mussles
  • Shrimps
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Red Wine

Instruction

  1. Cook Seafood with garlic in a pan
  2. Deglaze pan with Red Wine
  3. Add Tomato Sauce, herbs, and seasonings
  4. Bring to a boil, add noodles, and toss
